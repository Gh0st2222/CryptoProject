Every cycle that fresh candidate is run against the top vault champions re-validated on today's market and the running set, and whichever wins is hot-swapped into the live brains — so the best available champion drives trading, wherever it came from. The vault keeps the best & most-used 100 (most-used protected; never-used age out weekly). It never touches the settings you own.
